Role of Youth in Behavioural Transitions for Climate Action

Indira Basu, board member, IYCN, represented us at a panel discussion at TERI School Of Advanced Studies, on "Role of Youth in Behavioural Transitions for Climate Action". The panel, chaired by anthropologist Dr Swarup Dutta, included Dr Prodipto Ghosh, Former Secretary, MoEFCC, environmental economist, Ms Megha Nath, Dr Upasna Sharma, IIT-Delhi professor, and Richard Schmidt of Fridays for Future.
